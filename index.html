Asteroids

Arrow keys to move
Space to shoot
Press 's' to hyperspace
Press 'd' to shield
'w' to switch to guns
'x' to switch to missiles
'c' to switch to mines
Shift and Control to cycle through targets

DEBUG CONTROLS: (only use if you know what you're doing!)
'm' to spawn asteroids
'b' to spawn powerups
'n' to enable debug mode
